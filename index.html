How do you help organizations communicate better?

I help you find the right words and pictures to share what matters most. Think of me as a translator who takes your important work and makes it easy for everyone to understand - from partners, donors to community members.

Whether you're trying to explain a complex project or share a success story, I'll help you tell it in a way that connects with people's hearts and minds.

What makes your approach different?

I focus on listening first. Before I suggest any solutions, I take time to understand your unique situation, your audience, and what you're hoping to achieve.

Instead of using complicated theories, I offer practical tools your team can actually use. It's like having a friendly guide who shows you the path rather than just pointing at the destination.

How do you handle communication challenges?

When things get tricky - like when you need to share difficult news or correct misunderstandings - I help you find the kindest, clearest way forward.

I've worked through many sensitive situations and know how to balance honesty with care. Think of me as someone who helps you have hard conversations while keeping relationships strong.

What can I expect when we work together?

We'll start with a friendly conversation about your goals. Then I'll create a simple plan that shows exactly how we'll work together.

You'll always know what's happening next, and I'll make sure everything we create feels authentic to your organization. It's a partnership where your voice stays at the center.

How do you measure success?

Success looks different for everyone, so we'll define what matters most to you. It might be more people understanding your work, stronger relationships with donors, or your team feeling more confident about communication.

I use simple ways to track progress so you can see the difference our work is making.

What types of organizations do you work with?

I've worked with everyone from small community groups to large international organizations (INGOs). What matters isn't the size of your organization, but your commitment to making a difference.

If you have a story that needs telling or a message that needs sharing, I'd love to help you do it in a way that truly connects with people.

General Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer comprehensive media services including documentary production, photography (wedding, event, commercial), media strategy development, communication consulting, and training/mentorship for aspiring African storytellers.

Where are you based and do you travel?

I'm based in Kampala, Uganda, and serve clients across East Africa. Yes, I travel for projects throughout Uganda and neighboring countries. Travel expenses are calculated separately based on project requirements.

How long have you been doing this work?

I have over 10 years of experience in media production, communications consulting, and visual storytelling, working with both local and international organizations.

What makes your approach unique?

I combine professional media production with deep cultural understanding and strategic communication expertise. My focus is on authentic African storytelling that drives meaningful change.

Photography Services

What types of photography do you specialize in?

I specialize in wedding photography, event coverage, documentary photography, commercial/product photography, and portrait sessions. Each service is tailored to capture your unique story.

How far in advance should I book wedding photography?

I recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings, especially during peak seasons. However, I occasionally have last-minute availability, so it's always worth checking.

Do you offer engagement or pre-wedding shoots?

Yes! Engagement sessions are a great way to get comfortable in front of the camera before your wedding day. These can be booked separately or as part of wedding packages.

How long until I receive my photos?

For weddings: 4-6 weeks for full gallery delivery. For other sessions: 2-3 weeks. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you provide raw/unedited photos?

I deliver professionally edited, high-quality images. Raw files are not typically included but can be purchased separately for an additional fee.

Video Production

What types of videos do you produce?

Documentaries, promotional videos, event coverage, training videos, interview series, social media content, and corporate communications videos.

What is your production process?

My process includes: 1) Discovery and planning, 2) Pre-production (scripting, scheduling), 3) Production (filming), 4) Post-production (editing), and 5) Delivery and feedback.

How long does video production typically take?

Timelines vary by project complexity. A simple promotional video might take 2-3 weeks, while a documentary could take 2-3 months or more.

Do you offer drone videography?

Yes, I offer drone footage where appropriate and legally permissible. Additional permits and clearances may be required for certain locations.

Can you help with scriptwriting and story development?

Absolutely! Story development and scriptwriting are integral parts of my service. I work closely with clients to develop compelling narratives.

Media Strategy & Consulting

What kind of organizations do you work with?

I work with NGOs, international organizations, businesses, government agencies, educational institutions, and individual creators across Africa.

What does media strategy consulting involve?

It includes audience analysis, message development, content planning, platform strategy, performance measurement, and team capacity building.

Do you offer training workshops?

Yes, I offer workshops on smartphone filmmaking, visual storytelling, social media strategy, and communication skills for organizations and individuals.

Can you help with crisis communications?

Yes, I provide crisis communication planning and support, helping organizations navigate challenging situations with clear, compassionate messaging.

Pricing & Booking

How do you price your services?

Pricing is project-based and depends on scope, complexity, duration, and specific requirements. I provide custom quotes after understanding your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, mobile money (MTN, Airtel), and cash payments in Ugandan Shillings. International clients can pay via bank transfer in USD.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 50% deposit is required to secure your booking and begin work. The balance is due upon project completion.

What is your cancellation policy?

For photography sessions: Full refund if cancelled 14+ days in advance; 50% refund 7-14 days; no refund within 7 days. For other services, terms vary by project contract.

Do you offer packages or la carte services?

I offer both! I have standard packages for common services and can create custom packages based on your specific needs.

Technical Questions

What equipment do you use?

I use professional-grade cameras, lenses, lighting, and audio equipment from leading brands like Sony, Canon, and DJI. Specific equipment is chosen based on project needs.

Do you have backup equipment?

Yes, I always bring backup cameras, lenses, and essential equipment to ensure your project continues smoothly even if technical issues arise.

In what formats do you deliver files?

Photos: High-resolution JPEG files. Videos: MP4 files in various resolutions (up to 4K) based on your needs. I can also provide files optimized for specific platforms.

How do you handle file storage and backup?

All client files are backed up on multiple secure storage systems. I retain client projects for 5 years as part of my archival process.

Do you offer printing services for photos?

While I don't handle printing directly, I can recommend professional printing services and provide files optimized for print.

Collaboration & Process

How involved do I need to be in the process?

As involved as you'd like to be! Some clients prefer a hands-off approach while others want to be closely involved at every stage. I adapt to your preferred working style.

How many revisions are included?

Most projects include 2-3 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions are available for a reasonable additional fee.

Can I see work in progress?

Yes, I provide regular updates and can share drafts for feedback at key stages of the project.

Do you work with other creatives or subcontract?

For larger projects, I may collaborate with trusted specialists (second shooters, sound engineers, etc.) to ensure the best possible results.

How do you ensure cultural sensitivity in your work?

As an African storyteller deeply rooted in local contexts, I prioritize authentic, respectful representation and work closely with communities to ensure cultural accuracy.
